# Mood Ring — Color Framework

Each cycle is divided into four quarters. All four cycles share the same four-color palette, moving through the same spectrum. The color a cycle produces at any moment is determined entirely by where it sits in its quarter.

## Palette

| Quarter | Color | Hue |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Icy Blue | 218° (high lightness, pale) |
| Q2 | Green | 135° |
| Q3 | Warm Yellow | 50° |
| Q4 | Deep Purple | 290° (high saturation, low lightness) |

## Cycle Quarter Mapping

Aligned to Filippi's fractal model. All cycles share the same four-phase sequence.

| Quarter | Color | Neurotransmitter | Life arc | Moon | Solar (season) | Solar (24h) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Icy Blue | Acetylcholine | Youth | New moon | Spring | Morning (6am) |
| Q2 | Green | Serotonin | Young adult | First quarter | Summer | Midday (noon) |
| Q3 | Warm Yellow | Dopamine | Midlife | Full moon | Autumn | Afternoon (6pm) |
| Q4 | Deep Purple | Norepinephrine | Elder | Last quarter | Winter | Night (midnight) |

## Emotional Definitions (from Filippi)

| Quarter | Orientation | Quality |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 — Acetylcholine | Receptivity, seeding, bonding | Felt sense, emotional openness, returning to roots |
| Q2 — Serotonin | Concentration, cultivation, logic | Lucid, disciplined, building stamina |
| Q3 — Dopamine | Performance, arousal, expression | At peak, outward, reward-seeking |
| Q4 — Norepinephrine | Analysis, vigilance, reflection | Auditing, protective, conserving |

## Rules

- All cycles use the same four-color gradient, in the same order
- Cycles that repeat (moon, season, circadian) loop back from deep purple to icy blue
- Life arc does not loop — it terminates at deep purple
- Season cycle starts at spring equinox; circadian cycle starts at 6am
- Within each quarter, colors gradient smoothly into the next
- The blend of all four cycles at any moment is what the ring displays
